Welcome to the Prosim project

Prosim stands for PROduct SIMilarity.

The acronym OFF used in this documentation stands for OpenFoodFacts.

goal

This project aims at aggregating information delivered by the OpenFoodFacts collaborative database in order to deliver more or less similar products to a reference product of your choice, classified for instance by their nutrition score, but it could be enhanced by the community to take into account other criteria rgearding vegans, additives, allergens, etc.
